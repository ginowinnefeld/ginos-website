I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. The only data processing occurs through Plausible Analytics, which is GDPR compliant and based in Estonia (EU).

Contact Information

If you contact me via email or social media, I will only use your contact information to respond to your inquiry and will not add you to any mailing lists without explicit consent.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected (minimal anonymous analytics only)
  • Request deletion of any personal data
  • Opt out of analytics (block JavaScript or use browser privacy settings)

Cookies

This website does not use cookies. Plausible Analytics operates without cookies and respects Do Not Track settings.

Security

This website is served over HTTPS and follows modern security best practices. No personal data is stored on the website.

Changes to Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be updated on this page with a new "last updated" date.
